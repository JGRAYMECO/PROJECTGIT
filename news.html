15 February 2026

ARC Discovery Project Officially Commences

The Governing Immersive Tech Project has officially commenced under the Australian Research Council Discovery Program. Over the next three years, our interdisciplinary team will develop frameworks for proactive governance of immersive technologies.

The project will focus on harm prevention, corporate power, and regulatory preparedness in emerging extended reality systems.
