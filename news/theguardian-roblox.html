Professor Marcus Carter featured in The Guardian investigation into child safety on Roblox

Professor Marcus Carter has been featured as a key expert voice in an investigative piece by The Guardian which examined the safety of children on Roblox.

In the article, Carter likens Roblox’s loot boxes and casino games to ‘child gambling’, exposes how ‘pay to troll’ mechanics monetise bullying, and argues that Roblox exploits the creative labour of its young users—a phenomenon he terms ‘playbour’.
